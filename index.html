Mac and Cheese

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: Family of four (or one hungry boi)
Ingredients Quantity
Macaroni noodles 2 cups
Milk 1/2 cups
Butter 2 tablespoos
Shredded cheddar 1 cup
Bread crumbs 1/4 cup

Step 1: Boil 4 cups of water in a large sauce pan.
Step 2:Once water is rolling, place noodles in and cook for about 7 minutes or until tender.
Step 3: Drain noodles by placing them into a collinder.
Step 4:Turn heat to low and melt butter in the pot.
Step 5:Add cheese and milk to metled butter and stir until smooth.
Step 6:Add noodles back into the pot and mix until evenly coated, then transfer to oven safe dish.
Step 7:Top with bread crumbs and bake at 350 for 7 minutes or until golden brown.

Source: THE BEST HOMEMADE BAKED MAC AND CHEESE

Kettle Corn

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2-3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Vegetable Oil 1/4 cup
Unpopped popcorn kernels 1/2 cup
White sugar 1/4 cup

Step 1:Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat.
Step 2:Once hot, stir in the sugar and popcorn.
Step 3:Cover, and shake the pot constantly to keep the sugar from burning.
Step 4:Once the popping has slowed to once every 2 to 3 seconds, remove the pot from the heat and continue to shake for a few minutes until the popping has stopped.
Step 5:Pour into a large bowl, and allow to cool, stirring occasionally to break up large clumps.

Source: KETTLE CORN

Finger Caprese Salad

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Grape Tomatoes 1 cup
Basil leaves 1/2 cup
Fresh mozzeralla 15-20 small balls
Balsamic vinegar 1 fl oz

Step 1: Wash all ingredients thoroughly.
Step 2: Skewer one tomato, a basil leaf, and a mozzeralla ball onto a toothpick.
Step 3: Repeat step 2 until ingredients are used up. Step 3: Douse the skewers with the vinegar to preferred taste or leave it as an optional dip on the side.

Source: FINGER CAPRESE SALAD
